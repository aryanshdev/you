Why Use Strong Passwords?

Using strong password means using a password that is difficult for others to guess or crack. This helps protect your social accounts, bank accounts and information from others and help prevent fraud, and other bad things from happening to you.

Creating Strong Passwords

Use Longer Passwords

Longer Passwords are harder to crack. Ideal password length is 12 to 15 characters

Use Numbers and Symbols

Combining Alphabets, Numbers and Special Characters make it harder to guess and crack the password

Don't Use Personal Info

Don't use your birthdate, phone number, pet's name etcetera as your passwords as they can be guessed
